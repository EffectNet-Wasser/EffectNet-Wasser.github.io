Acceptance

The main task of the social sciences team headed by Prof. Dr. Jale Tosun is to share the insights gained from scientific research with the greater public audience. Still, our goals are not confined to a unilateral transfer of knowledge. Rather, our researchers are eager both to engage with various stakeholders, and to derive specific as well as practical recommendations for improving the sustainability of aquatic ecosystems.

Among others, these include such concepts as

  • encouraging producers to use less harmful chemicals,
  • attracting consumers’ attention and causing them to reduce their usage rate of drugs, pharmaceuticals and food additives,
  • assisting environmental groups and educational institutions to inform the public about potential hazards, and
  • supporting policy makers to implement effective regulatory measures.
